All new employees should complete onboarding training within their first week. Training materials are available on the LMS portal.
Pre-Onboarding: Send offer letter, collect documents, and set up accounts and workspace.

Orientation: Introduce company, policies, teams, and culture.

Role Training: Explain responsibilities, tools, and initial tasks; assign a mentor.

Integration: Involve in projects, meetings, and team activities.

Continuous Learning: Provide ongoing training and skill development.

Evaluation & Feedback: Gather employee and manager feedback to improve onboarding.
